Mason's Eagle Project

Hello, I have created an eagle project and am accepting sign-ups now

What is the project?

I am doing an Eagle Project with the Manoa Community Garden (MCG). The MCG is the garden right next to the Scout Hut. The MCG's compost bin is decaying, and I have planned to get it replaced.

Steps we will be doing:

  • Painting the plastic pallets
  • Adding mesh to the new pallets
  • Moving the compost off of the old wooden pallets
  • Removing the old wooden pallets
  • Putting in the new pallets
  • Moving the compost back
  • Going to the dump to dump the old pallets

What are the details?

My project will be on May 13th. You do not need to bring anything, tools and safety will be provided. Please wear/apply gear for a day in the hot sun.

We will be meeting behind the Scout Hut
